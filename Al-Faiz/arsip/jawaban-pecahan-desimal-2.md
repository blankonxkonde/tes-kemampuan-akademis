## Halaman 181

**23. Pembahasan:**
$$\left(1+\frac{7}{8}\right) \div \left(2-\frac{1}{4}\right) \times \left(\frac{3}{5}-2\right)$$
$$= \left(\frac{8}{8}+\frac{7}{8}\right) \div \left(\frac{8}{4}-\frac{1}{4}\right) \times \left(\frac{3}{5}-\frac{10}{5}\right)$$
$$= \frac{15}{8} \div \frac{7}{4} \times \left(-\frac{7}{5}\right)$$
$$= \frac{15}{8} \times \frac{4}{7} \times \left(-\frac{7}{5}\right)$$
$$= \frac{3}{8} \times 4 \times (-1)$$
$$= -\frac{3}{2}$$
**Jawaban: B**

$$= 1\frac{1}{12}$$

Maka $0,200 = 0,2$
**Jawaban: B**

**30. Pembahasan:**
$$(234 - 13)^2 - (161 + 59)^2$$
$$= (221)^2 - (220)^2$$
